1 Online Check-in

Check-in that happens before guests arrive

Guests complete the whole process online, on their own phone, before they set foot on the property, with no app to download. They fill in their details, verify their identity, and handle any payment or deposit in one guided flow. You get a verified, ready-to-go reservation without the front-desk back-and-forth.

How it works for the host

You send (or auto-send) the check-in link. The guest completes each step at their own pace, and you see the status of every reservation at a glance, stepping in only when something needs your attention. It's easier than ever: guest details are captured with ID OCR, which scans the document and pulls the relevant information to fill in the form automatically.

2 Identity Verification

Know who's really staying, before they arrive

Already mandatory in some countries and always recommended everywhere. Guests scan their ID document and take a quick selfie or video with liveness detection. The system checks that the document is valid and that the person matches it, so you can trust that the guest checking in is who they claim to be. It works with passports, national IDs, and driver's licenses from around the world.

Why it matters

Fewer fraudulent bookings, safer properties, and a check-in that feels modern instead of a hassle. You can even require verification to be completed before the guest can continue.

3 Security Deposit

Protect your property without charging your guests upfront

Collect a security deposit as part of the online check-in, with no awkward cash handling or manual card requests. You choose how it works:

  • 🔒 Pre-authorization: the deposit amount is held on the guest's card but not charged. It's simply blocked until the stay is over, then released automatically. If there's damage, you decide whether to capture it.
  • € Full charge: the amount is collected directly, for cases where you prefer to take payment rather than hold it.
Why it matters

Guests feel comfortable because their money isn't actually taken, and you stay protected in case anything goes wrong. The whole thing happens inside the same check-in flow the guest is already completing.
